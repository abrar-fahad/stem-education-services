Services

Practical STEM services for programs that need more than a flashy flyer.

Curriculum, lesson plans, robotics implementation, instructor support, competitions, and hands-on technology programming.

01

Curriculum Development

Structured curriculum for coding, Robotics, AI, Web Development, App Development, Game Development, Data Science, and project-based STEM learning.

Deliverables can include:

  • Scope and sequence documents
  • Weekly lesson plans and pacing guides
  • Student project briefs and teacher guides
  • Assessments, rubrics, and progress tracking tools
  • Beginner-to-advanced coding pathways
02

Program Introduction & Launch

Support for schools and organizations introducing new STEM programs with clear structure, resources, expectations, and student outcomes.

Best for:

  • New after-school STEM programs
  • School coding clubs
  • Summer or holiday camps
  • Technology enrichment tracks
  • Parent-facing program information sessions
03

Robotics in Your School

Robotics programs that connect engineering, coding, problem-solving, teamwork, sensors, motors, and hands-on building.

Program elements:

  • LEGO SPIKE, EV3, VEX, Robotis STEM, and drone coding concepts
  • Build challenges and engineering design cycles
  • Robot maze, rescue, race, and obstacle challenges
  • Classroom management and equipment planning
  • Student showcase or competition format
04

Instructor Training & Program Quality

Training support for instructors who need to teach technical subjects clearly, manage student progress, and deliver consistent lesson quality.

Training areas:

  • Lesson delivery and classroom flow
  • Student engagement strategies
  • Project troubleshooting and debugging support
  • Parent communication and progress updates
  • Assessment and reflection routines
05

Competitions, Hack Nights & Showcases

Event design for students to build, present, compete, collaborate, and develop confidence through meaningful STEM experiences.

Examples:

  • Robotics challenge day
  • Beginner coding competition
  • AI idea pitch showcase
  • Game development demo night
  • Math + logic challenge tournament
06

Lesson Plans & Resource Packs

Ready-to-use resources for technical instructors, schools, and programs that need structured lessons without rebuilding everything from scratch.

Can include:

  • Slide outlines and teaching notes
  • Starter code and challenge tasks
  • Exit tickets and short quizzes
  • Project extension ideas
  • Parent-friendly program summaries
