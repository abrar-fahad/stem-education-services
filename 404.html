What I help build

From idea to implementation, without turning education into a paperwork swamp.

My work blends technical instruction, curriculum development, program management, and student-centered learning. I design experiences where students build real projects: robots that move, games that work, websites that publish, AI models that make predictions, and math concepts that finally feel useful.

The goal is not to add technology for decoration. The goal is to create programs with structure, outcomes, engagement, and clear learning growth.
