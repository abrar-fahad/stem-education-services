What I help build

From idea to implementation, with structure students and teachers can actually use.

My work brings together technical instruction, curriculum development, program design, and student-centered learning. I create STEM experiences where students do more than listen. They build robots, design games, publish websites, create apps, explore AI models, solve math challenges, and present real projects.

The goal is not to add technology just because it sounds impressive on a flyer. The goal is to build programs with clear outcomes, strong lesson flow, hands-on activities, teacher-ready materials, and meaningful student growth.
